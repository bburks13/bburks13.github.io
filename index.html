TIP

Experience our museum's remarkable car collection by exploring the building like a parking garage. Walk through each floor, taking the right-side ramps. Once you reach the NAPA auto center on the bottom floor, circle upwards using the left ramps.
Each ramp is a specially themed exhibit, avoid elevators & stairs to ensure you don't miss any cars.

MAKE THE MOST OF YOUR VISIT
  • To keep our cars safe, NO TOUCHING except in the Family Zone and Speed Zone
  • We encourage photos, but no selfie sticks, tripods, or large backpacks
  • Our cars are on a strict diet. No food or drink outside of the cafe or store area
  • Video surveillance is in use throughout the Museum


  • We strongly recommend you wear a cloth face covering while enjoying our exhibits
  • Please practice social distancing by keeping one car length between you and the group ahead of you
